Road Map

Stay up-to-date on the development and track updates of the Gemly economic strategy here or in our Telegram channel.
14 February
Gemly Game Launch
The long-awaited day when Gemly opens its doors for the first players. This means that all systems and game mechanics, which the team has worked on for many months, are finally ready for use.
19 February
Global Contest Start
Preparation and launch of a balanced gaming contest, in which all game users can participate to compete for valuable prizes.
21 February
Bounty Program
A special bounty program to encourage active Gemly players for their gaming achievements and actions that are beneficial for its development.
24 February
Mobile Application
Publication of the official mobile application of Gemly's incremental economic strategy for the Android platform.
Winter 2023
Level & Boosters System
Implementation of a full-fledged level and experience system, which will later become the foundation for further updates of game mechanics based on it. Introduction of boosters — performance accelerators.
Winter 2023
Personal Messages
The first stage in the development of social relationships within Gemly is a personal messaging system for communication between players, technical support, and especially important system notifications.
Winter 2023
Modifiers & Leaders
Introduction of modifiers capable of positively impacting the gaming experience depending on different gaming conditions. Implementation of a leader system in various categories.
1 March
Spring 2023 Road Map
The spring 2023 road map, a review of future updates, and the plan for further development will be published on March 1st, 2023.

24-02-2023 at 21:00

Mobile Application

We are excited to present to you the official Gemly app, currently available only for Android systems. You can already download our APK from the official website and soon from the Google Play Store. The mobile app for iOS systems is still under development, so subscribe to our news channel to be the first to know when it is released.
