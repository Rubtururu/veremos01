Dear friends! We are pleased to introduce our bounty program. «Quests» is a place where you can earn gems for specific actions, such as publishing videos on YouTube or in-game achievements such as referrals, cycles, killed monsters, and so on. In this news post, we will talk about the types of different tasks in «Quests».

How do «Quests» work?

Quests are a new way to earn gems in Gemly that absolutely doesn't require any investment from you. You can find them in the account menu under «Earn» → «Quests». We have added for you in advance 13 different tasks, some of which can be completed with little effort, while others, as a rule, the highest paid, may require certain actions from you.

Check out all available quests, maybe by the time you read this news, you have already completed one of the necessary conditions to receive a reward in one of them. By the way, the gems received as a result of completing tasks are immediately available for payout.

Repeatable tasks

Repeatable tasks are also different from all the rest in that their completion is manually checked by the Gemly administration. This takes no more than 24 hours.Repeatable tasks differ from ordinary ones in that they can be completed several times, for example, the task of publishing a payment can be done once every 5 days. In the future, we will add more repeatable tasks, but at the moment there are only two of them:

  • Posting of payout
  • Video review

Other types of tasks

All other tasks, except repeatable ones, are checked by the system automatically. As soon as you complete the conditions for a particular task, confidently click the «Receive» button — the reward will be instantly credited to your gem balance.

Changelog as of February 21

  • Gemly has an official community for players from Brazil, you can join it through these links: Players chat / News.
  • Now for convenience, when paying to a payment system with which you have already made a payment before, the details that were previously used are inserted in advance.
  • Added information on the total amount spent on mercenaries in the game statistics window.
  • Fixed the online counter, which broke when the number of players online exceeded 1000.
  • Fixed the issue of incorrect display of the loot list in the game.
  • Fixed an error when editing a running link in «Website surfing».

Now you know what the bounty program looks like in Gemly — «Quests». It's time to try to complete one of the tasks. In the future, we plan to expand the range of tasks available for completion. And for now, that's all, see you in the game!
